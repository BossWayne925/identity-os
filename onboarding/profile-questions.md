# Deep Profile Questions

Optional enrichment, suggested after the first week of coaching. 12 sections, 100+ questions — run as conversation, a section or two at a sitting, not as homework. There is no separate profile file: answers fold into the existing `me/` files (`identity-now`, `becoming`, `values-stated`, `weaknesses`, `triggers`) with the user's approval, sharpening what intake started.

No right answers. No performance. Just truth. The coach asks one question at a time, reflects answers back in sharper language, and proposes the `me/` file updates at the end of each section.

---

## 1. Identity

*Who you are vs. who you're becoming.*

**Who are you right now, honestly?**


**Who are you becoming? Describe that person in 2-3 sentences.**


**What does the best version of you look like in 5 years?**


**What identity are you shedding? Who are you leaving behind?**


**What identity are you stepping into? What does that person do differently?**


**When do you feel most like yourself?**


**What do people who know you best say about you?**


**What do you wish they said?**


**What's one belief about yourself you know you need to update?**


**What label or identity has held you back the longest?**


---

## 2. Values

*What you actually live by — not what you say you believe.*

**What are your top 5 values?**


**Which of those do you consistently live by? Which ones are aspirational?**


**When did you last compromise a value? What happened?**


**What would you never trade for money?**


**What makes you feel most in alignment?**


**What makes you feel most out of alignment?**


**What does integrity mean to you specifically — not in theory, in practice?**


**What would you regret NOT doing with your life?**


---

## 3. Mindset & Manifestation

*Your beliefs, what you're rewiring, and what you're calling in.*

**Do you believe your identity can fundamentally change? What's your evidence?**


**What limiting beliefs do you carry about your own potential?**


**What do you tell yourself when you fail or fall short?**


**Do you believe you can have what you actually want? Where does the doubt live?**


**What does visualization mean to you personally? How do you currently use it?**


**What would you manifest if you knew it was guaranteed?**


**What story do you keep telling yourself that needs to die?**


**What would you do if you knew you absolutely could not fail?**


**Write your current relationship with affirmations — does it feel real or forced?**


**What does "becoming" feel like to you? Exciting? Scary? Both?**


---

## 4. Habits & Consistency

*The real patterns — not the intended ones.*

**What habits define your current daily life (good and bad)?**


**What habit has the highest ROI that you're not doing consistently?**


**What habit is costing you the most and you already know it?**


**When in your life have you been most consistent? What was different then?**


**What time of day are you most naturally disciplined?**


**What is your biggest consistency killer?**


**What excuse do you use most often when you miss?**


**What does your morning actually look like right now?**


**What would your ideal morning look like?**


**What's the gap between those two mornings? What's in the way?**


---

## 5. Focus & Productivity

*Where your time actually goes.*

**Where does your time go vs. where you want it to go?**


**What is your single biggest distraction?**


**How long can you focus without interruption before you break?**


**What environments help you do your best work?**


**What does a productive day feel like vs. what it actually looks like most days?**


**What tasks do you avoid most? Why?**


**What's always on your list that never gets done?**


**If you had 2 extra focused hours every day, what would you use them for?**


---

## 6. Health

*Physical, mental, and everything in between.*

**How do you rate your physical health right now (1–10) and why?**


**What is your honest relationship with food?**


**How is your sleep — quality, quantity, consistency?**


**What does your movement/exercise reality look like vs. your intention?**


**What does your body need that you're not giving it?**


**How do you handle stress physically? What does it do to your body?**


**What is your mental health status right now, honestly?**


**What does feeling physically well make possible for you that currently isn't?**


---

## 7. Learning

*What you're consuming and what you're actually applying.*

**What are you currently learning or studying?**


**What do you want to learn that you haven't started yet?**


**What is your best learning method — reading, listening, doing, watching?**


**What's the gap between what you consume and what you implement?**


**What topic could you talk about for 3 hours without stopping?**


**What skill would change your life most if you actually developed it?**


**What are you avoiding learning because it makes you uncomfortable?**


**Where do you consume most of your content, and is it actually helping?**


---

## 8. Content & Brand

*Your voice, your audience, and what you want to be known for.*

**Do you currently create content? What kind?**


**What do you want to be known for?**


**Who is your audience, or who do you want it to be?**


**What is your authentic voice — how do you talk when you're not performing?**


**What's your biggest fear about putting yourself out there more?**


**What content have you made or shared that felt most true to you?**


**What do you want to say that you haven't said yet?**


**What's stopping you from being more visible?**


---

## 9. Business

*What you're building and why.*

**What are you building right now?**


**What problem does it solve, and for who specifically?**


**What does success look like for this in 12 months?**


**What is your biggest business blocker right now?**


**What would you build if money was completely off the table?**


**What have you tried that hasn't worked? What did you learn?**


**What is the one thing that would move the needle most right now?**


**What are you avoiding in your business that you know you need to face?**


---

## 10. Goals

*90 days, 1 year, 5 years — with first actions and honest obstacles.*

**What is your #1 goal for the next 90 days?**


**What is the first concrete action toward it?**


**What is the biggest obstacle in the way?**


**What is your #1 goal for the next 12 months?**


**What does the identity of the person who achieves it look like?**


**What is your 5-year vision — life, work, relationships, health?**


**What goal have you been carrying for more than 2 years without completing?**


**What goal are you afraid to say out loud? Say it here.**


**What would make this year feel like a real success?**


**What would you accomplish if you committed fully for just 90 days?**


---

## 11. Coaching Preferences

*How your coach should work with you specifically.*

**How do you want to be challenged — gentle push or hard confrontation?**


**What kind of feedback shuts you down vs. opens you up?**


**How do you handle being pushed when you don't want to hear it?**


**What do you need from a coach that you've never actually gotten?**


**What do coaches or mentors usually get wrong with you?**


**When you drift or lose focus, how do you want to be called back?**


**What is the one thing a coach should never do with you?**


**How honest do you want your coach to be on a scale of 1–10? Explain.**


---

## 12. Self Awareness

*The questions most people skip. Don't skip them.*

**What are you most afraid to admit about yourself?**


**What pattern keeps repeating in your life no matter what you do?**


**What do other people see in you that you struggle to see in yourself?**


**What do you hide from most people?**


**What version of yourself are you most ashamed of?**


**What is the bravest thing you have ever done?**


**What would you tell the version of you from 5 years ago?**


**What is the biggest lie you tell yourself on a regular basis?**


---

*Section 11 (Coaching Preferences) is special: if answers here contradict the persona built during intake, offer to re-run the persona builder with the new information.*
