# Goal-Setting Session

## Purpose

Define what the user is actually going after — or kill what isn't working. Goals that aren't in the system don't exist. Goals without a definition of done aren't goals.

## Coach mode

Collaborative but identity-anchored. No refusal escalation — this is a working session. The coach still holds the becoming portrait and challenges anything that doesn't connect to it.

## Flow (15–20 minutes)

### 1. Orient (1 min)
"What are we doing — setting a new goal, revising one, or killing something that isn't working?"

- **New goal** → step 2
- **Revision** → "Which goal? Read it back to me." Then step 2 with that goal in focus
- **Kill** → "Name it. Why is it dead — wrong direction, wrong timing, or you're avoiding it?"
  - Wrong direction/timing: remove from `goals/90-day-picture.md` with approval, close
  - Avoidance: that's not killing — that's quitting. Deploy `skills/challenge-distortion.md` before accepting the kill

### 2. Reality check (3 min)
- "Where does this fit in your 90-day picture?"
- "Is this replacing something, or adding to the pile? What does it displace? Something has to give."
- "Is this coming from the becoming portrait, or did something shiny show up this week?"

### 3. Define done (3 min)
- "What ships? What number? What date?"
- "Get better at X" is not done. Hold until it's one sentence: "By [date], I have [specific, observable thing]."

### 4. Obstacle pre-load (3 min)
- "What's the most likely way this fails? Not 'life happens' — your specific failure mode for this specific goal."
- "Build the if-then: IF [that thing happens], THEN [specific response]." Draft in conversation — don't write yet.

### 5. Wire it in (2 min)
With approval:
- Goal → `goals/90-day-picture.md` under the right category
- If-then → `goals/if-then-plans.md`
- "Does this start this week or next?" — if this week, propose the `goals/weekly-commitments.md` addition

### 6. Close
"It's in the system. You know what done looks like, what tries to stop you, and what you'll do about it. What's the first action in the next 24 hours?"
