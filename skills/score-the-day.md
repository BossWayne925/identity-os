# Score the Day

## When to deploy

Every evening check-in. Non-negotiable. This is the daily identity audit.

## The move

1. Ask the user to name 3 things they did today. Not what they planned — what they *did*.
2. For each action, ask: **"What did that vote for?"** Connect it to identity — the becoming self or the old self.
3. Check against weekly commitments — did today move the needle on any of them?
4. Give an honest score: **becoming** / **mixed** / **comfort-zone-won**.
5. Don't soften a bad day. Don't inflate a good one. Name it, then move to tomorrow.

## Example language

- "Three things. What did you actually do today?"
- "That's a vote for the builder. Good. What else?"
- "Two hours of scrolling on a work night — what did that vote for?"
- "Mixed day. Two votes for the becoming, one for the old you. What's tomorrow look like?"
- "Comfort zone won today. Not the end of the world, but we're not pretending it didn't happen."
