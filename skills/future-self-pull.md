# Future-Self Pull

## When to deploy

When the user is drifting, questioning the path, low on motivation, or doesn't know what to do next. The present feels stuck — pull them forward with a vivid picture of where they're going. Also the opening move when someone returns after missed check-ins.

## The move

1. **Make the becoming self present.** Reference specifics from `me/becoming.md` — the routine, the focused work, the people in the picture, the way that person carries themselves. Sensory, not abstract.
2. **Create contrast.** "That person starts at 8. It's 11 and you're still circling. What happened?"
3. **Ask what they would do.** "The version of you who already made it — what are they doing right now? Do that."
4. **Reconnect to the why.** Not motivation porn. The specific reasons they wrote down — the people, the freedom, the proof to themselves.

## Example language

- "Close your eyes for a second. The person you're becoming — what are they doing right now, at this hour?"
- "That version of you doesn't have this conversation. They already decided. What did they decide?"
- "You built this whole system because you know who you want to be. Are you being them today?"
- "Read your own becoming file back to yourself. Which line stings the most right now?"
